Connections that Drive Business

At the Rosario Chamber of Commerce, we foster the growth of our business community through strategic meetings and networking. Join a space where entrepreneurs, professionals and companies collaborate, share ideas and generate opportunities. Together, we build alliances that strengthen local development and boost the success of each business.

A Year of Achievements and Business Growth

Last year marked a milestone in the economic growth of the companies that are part of the Rosario Chamber of Commerce. Thanks to strategic initiatives, key alliances and the commitment of our business network, we have promoted projects that generated employment, innovations and expansion in various sectors. This success would not be possible without the collective effort of our members. Join us and be part of this movement that transforms businesses and strengthens our local economy.

Exclusive Benefits to Boost Your Business

At the Rosario Chamber of Commerce, we offer you more than just a membership: we offer you tools to transform your business. Access personalized advice, exclusive networking spaces, and visibility opportunities that will position you as a leader in your sector. In addition, enjoy unique benefits such as discounts on services, training for you and your team, and access to strategic information. Join our community and start taking advantage of everything you need to take your company to the next level.

Committed to a Sustainable Future

At the Rosario Chamber of Commerce, we believe that business success must go hand in hand with caring for the environment. That is why we launched the community project 'Rosario Verde', an initiative that brings together companies, entrepreneurs and citizens in concrete actions to reduce our environmental footprint. From recycling campaigns to reforestation of public spaces, our goal is to build a more sustainable city for future generations.

Gateway to International Trade

The port of Rosario is a key pillar for international trade, connecting our city with global markets. Thanks to its strategic location and modern infrastructure, it allows for the efficient export and import of goods, strengthening the local economy and boosting business opportunities for Rosario companies. Join the Chamber and access resources and contacts that will help you take full advantage of the potential of this logistics hub
